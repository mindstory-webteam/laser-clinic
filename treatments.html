Complete Treatment Guide

Every concern needs a personalised plan

These cards explain our major treatment categories. During consultation, the doctor assesses your skin or hair condition and recommends the safest, most suitable procedure plan.

Laser Dermatology

Laser Treatments

Laser treatments use focused light energy to treat selected skin concerns with accuracy. They are commonly used for pigmentation, acne scars, moles, birth marks, tattoo removal and different types of scars.

Helps With

  • Moles
  • Birth marks
  • Acne scars
  • Other scars
  • Pigmentation
  • Tattoo removal

Approach

The doctor examines the skin and selects the right laser type and settings. Multiple sessions may be advised depending on the concern and response.

Clearer Skin

Acne & Scar Care

Acne and acne scars need a customised approach. Active breakouts are controlled with medical management, while acne scars may require procedures to improve skin texture and reduce visible scar depth.

Includes

  • Medical management
  • Chemical peels
  • MNRF
  • Lasers
  • Threads

Ideal For

Active acne, acne marks, post-acne pigmentation, open pores, uneven skin texture, depressed scars and recurring breakouts.

Glow & Even Tone

Skin Lightening & Brightening

Skin brightening treatments help improve dull skin, tanning, uneven tone, pigmentation and dark patches. Treatment may include peels, lasers, glutathione injections and mesotherapy depending on the concern.

Includes

  • Chemical peels
  • Lasers
  • Glutathione injections
  • Mesotherapy
  • Face brightening
  • Body brightening

Treatment Areas

Face, body, selected intimate areas for females, underarms / axillae and other areas after doctor consultation.

Scalp & Hair Health

Hair Loss Treatment

Hair loss treatment starts by identifying the reason for hair fall or thinning. The plan may include medicines, PRP, GFC, mesotherapy, lasers and microblading to support scalp health and improve density over time.

Includes

  • Medical management
  • PRP
  • GFC
  • Mesotherapy
  • Lasers
  • Microblading

Ideal For

Hair fall, thinning hair, weak roots, reduced density, scalp-related hair concerns and selected eyebrow enhancement needs.

Rejuvenation

Anti-Aging Treatments

Anti-aging treatments help refresh the skin and improve visible signs of aging. These procedures can soften fine lines, improve firmness, restore volume and enhance glow while maintaining natural-looking results.

Includes

  • GFC
  • Threads
  • Botox
  • Fillers
  • HIFU
  • Lasers
  • Mesotherapy

Helps With

Fine lines, wrinkles, sagging, dullness, uneven texture, facial volume loss and early signs of aging.

Texture & Clarity

Chemical Peels

Chemical peels gently exfoliate the upper layers of the skin to improve clarity, brightness and texture. Different peel types and strengths are selected according to acne, pigmentation, tanning, dullness or sensitivity.

Helps With

  • Acne
  • Acne marks
  • Pigmentation
  • Tanning
  • Dull skin
  • Uneven skin tone

Procedure Note

Peel type and strength are decided after skin examination. Sun protection and post-care are important for better results.

Skin Polishing

Microdermabrasion

Microdermabrasion is a gentle skin polishing treatment that removes dead skin cells and improves surface smoothness. It is commonly used for dull skin, mild roughness, superficial marks and uneven appearance.

Helps With

  • Dull skin
  • Rough texture
  • Mild marks
  • Surface buildup
  • Uneven appearance

Ideal For

People looking for smoother, cleaner and refreshed-looking skin with minimal downtime.

Firmness & Lift

Skin Tightening & Wrinkle Reduction

Skin tightening and wrinkle reduction treatments help improve loose skin, fine lines and loss of firmness. Depending on the concern, the doctor may recommend HIFU, threads, lasers, Botox, fillers or other rejuvenation treatments.

Helps With

  • Loose skin
  • Fine lines
  • Wrinkles
  • Facial laxity
  • Loss of firmness

Treatment Goal

To improve firmness, refresh skin quality and support a younger, natural-looking appearance.

Cosmetic Enhancement

Botox, Fillers & Threads

Botox, fillers and threads are used for selected cosmetic concerns such as wrinkles, volume loss, facial sagging and contour enhancement. Treatments are planned carefully to maintain facial balance and natural expression.

Includes

  • Botox
  • Dermal fillers
  • Thread lift
  • Facial contouring
  • Wrinkle softening

Ideal For

Fine lines, wrinkles, smile lines, facial sagging, volume loss and selected contouring needs.

Brow Design

Microblading

Microblading is a brow enhancement procedure that uses fine pigment strokes to improve eyebrow shape and fullness. It is suitable for selected people who want natural-looking brows with better definition.

Helps With

  • Thin eyebrows
  • Uneven brow shape
  • Sparse brows
  • Brow definition

Procedure Note

Suitability depends on skin type, brow condition and doctor consultation.

Other Dermatology Treatments

Other Services
