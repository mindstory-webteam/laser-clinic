Treatments explained

Treatments Explained

A closer look at each treatment category — what it does, who it's for, and how we approach it.

Laser Treatments

Focused laser energy is used for pigmentation, acne scars, moles, birth marks, tattoo removal and other scars. The treatment choice depends on the skin condition and a doctor consultation.

Acne & Scar Care

A multi-step approach for active pimples and scars using medical management, chemical peels, MNRF, lasers and threads. Plans are customised based on acne grade, skin type and scar depth.

Skin Lightening Treatment

Skin brightening and even-tone treatments using chemical peels, lasers, glutathione injections and mesotherapy. Areas may include face, body, axillae and selected intimate areas for females after consultation.

Hair Loss Treatment

Comprehensive hair fall treatment using medical management, PRP, GFC, scalp mesotherapy, lasers and microblading. Treatment depends on the cause of hair loss and scalp condition.

Anti-Aging Treatment

Non-surgical rejuvenation including botulinum toxin (eg. Botox), fillers, threads, HIFU, GFC, peels, lasers, mesotherapy and glutathione. Helps improve wrinkles, volume loss, skin laxity, dullness and texture.

Micropigmentation & Threads

Micropigmentation enhances eyebrow shape using semi-permanent pigment strokes. Thread treatments help lift and tighten selected facial areas. Suitability depends on consultation.
